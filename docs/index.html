VISION

1 - Use cases


Maria Amaral: Maria is 22 years old, and she is waiting for his plane at the airport. She checks the interactive map/table to check where his plane is at that point


Elon Rodrigues: Elon is 35 years old, and his flighting his airplane. He checks some menu to verify if there is some mayday airport available at the moment.


Jessica Lauren: Jessica has 3 children, and she is a little busy at the airport while taking care of his sons and luggage. She sets an alarm to know when his flight is about to take off, so that she doesn’t take the risk to lose his flight.


Lee White: Lee is waiting for his friend that comes in flight xyz. He is going to search if the flight xyz has no delay.


Lily Madison: Lily has nothing to do at the airport, so she checks some info/statistics/curiosities about her flight.


Wilson: Wilson is a cabby. He is notified when a plane is about to land in his city airport.
